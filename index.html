My favourite Music artist:

My most favourite music artist is Melanie Martinez. She produces songs and music videos that look very pastel and cutesy on the outside, but touch on very deep themes that I resonate with a lot.

I first discovered Mel in high school, when I actually began listening to music and have been a long-term fan for about 6 years.




The "Portals" Album:

Her latest album is titled "Portals" and it continues the story set in her last album "K-12" where her character dies in the end, and now she has been reincarnated as a kind of "fairy" or "moth-like creature"
